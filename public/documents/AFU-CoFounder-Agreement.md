## 8. DEPARTURE AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 Voluntary Departure

If a Co-Founder voluntarily departs:
- **Unvested equity** is forfeited and returns to the company pool
- **Vested equity** is retained by the departing Co-Founder
- The company has a **Right of First Refusal** to purchase vested equity at Fair Market Value
- 90-day transition period for knowledge transfer

### 8.2 Termination for Cause

A Co-Founder may be terminated for Cause (fraud, gross negligence, material breach, conviction of a felony):
- **All unvested equity** is immediately forfeited
- **Vested equity** may be repurchased by the company at a 50% discount to Fair Market Value
- Departing Co-Founder must immediately return all company property and materials

### 8.3 Death or Incapacity

In the event of death or permanent incapacity:
- All equity shall be deemed **fully vested**
- The estate/representative has 12 months to sell equity back to the company at Fair Market Value
- Company has Right of First Refusal
