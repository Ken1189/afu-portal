# Content & Copy Quality Auditor

Audit every public-facing page for content quality, accuracy, and credibility.

## Pages to Audit

### Homepage (/)
1. Hero headline and subtitle — clear, compelling, accurate?
2. Stats/numbers — are they real or fake? Any showing "0"?
3. Services section — descriptions accurate to what's actually available?
4. Membership tiers — pricing consistent with /memberships page?
5. Partner logos — are these real partnerships or aspirational?
6. Testimonials — are these real people? Verifiable?
7. Investment section — are return claims appropriate for pre-revenue?
8. CTAs — do they all link to working pages?

### About (/about)
9. Leadership — only real people (Devon + Peter)?
10. Mission statement — clear and authentic?
11. Community programs — do the DONATE buttons work?
12. Claims about "20 countries" — accurate to current state?

### Services (all /services/* pages)
13. Each service page — does it describe something AFU actually offers today?
14. Pricing/rates mentioned — accurate?
15. "Apply" or "Get Started" buttons — do they work?
16. Any promises that can't be delivered yet?

### Investors (/investors)
17. Return projections (10-20%) — appropriate disclaimers?
18. "$500M seed round" — correct terminology?
19. Traction claims — match reality?
20. Pre-revenue disclosure — prominent enough?

### Countries (/countries/*)
21. Member counts — real or inflated?
22. "Active" vs "Planned" — clearly distinguished?
23. Crop data — accurate per country?

### Jobs (/jobs)
24. Number of open positions — realistic for current stage?
25. Job descriptions — professional and accurate?

### Memberships (/memberships)
26. Pricing matches homepage pricing?
27. Features listed — all actually available?
28. "Most Popular" badge — appropriate?

## What to flag
- **Fake/unverified claims** — things stated as fact that aren't proven
- **Inflated numbers** — stats that don't match reality
- **Broken promises** — features claimed but not built
- **Inconsistencies** — different numbers/facts on different pages
- **Placeholder text** — lorem ipsum, "coming soon", sample data
- **Missing disclaimers** — financial claims without risk warnings
- **Dead links** — CTAs pointing nowhere

## Output format

### Missing Disclaimers:

### Placeholder/Fake Content:
