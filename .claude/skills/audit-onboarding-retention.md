# Onboarding & Retention Auditor

Check the full user experience from first visit to active daily user. Identify where users drop off or get confused.

## First Visit Experience

### New Visitor (No Account)
1. Homepage loads fast? Clear value proposition above fold?
2. "Become a Member" CTA visible and prominent?
3. Can they understand what AFU does within 10 seconds?
4. Is the pricing clear before they sign up?
5. Social proof visible (farmer count, country count)?

### Application Flow (/apply)
6. How many fields? (should be <10 for first step)
7. Is there a progress indicator?
8. Does it explain what happens after application?
9. Estimated wait time for approval?
10. Confirmation message clear?

### Waiting Period (Application Submitted → Approved)
11. Does the farmer get any communication while waiting?
12. Is there a status check page?
13. How long does approval typically take?

## First Login Experience

### Dashboard (/dashboard)
14. Is there a welcome message for new users?
15. Onboarding checklist visible? (profile, KYC, first crop, etc.)
16. Are empty states helpful? (not just blank pages)
17. Quick start guide or tutorial?
18. Most important action highlighted?

### Farm Portal (/farm)
19. Seedling tier — what's visible? Is it enough to be useful?
20. How to unlock more features — is it clear?
21. First crop entry — guided or confusing?
22. AI assistant visible and accessible?

## Daily Use Hooks

### What brings farmers back daily?
23. Market prices — are they updating? Real or static?
24. Weather alerts — configured and sending?
25. Crop health monitoring — satellite/AI working?
26. Wallet balance — easy to check?
27. Notifications — are in-app notifications working?

### Retention Mechanisms
28. Training courses — can they earn certificates?
29. Achievement system — badges working?
30. Tier progression — clear path from Seedling → Pioneer?
31. Community features — cooperatives active?
32. Marketplace — products available to browse?

## Supplier First Experience

### After Approval
33. Dashboard — clear what to do first?
34. Add first product — is it guided?
35. Marketplace visible with their products?
36. Analytics — anything to see on day 1?

## Ambassador First Experience

### After Approval
37. Dashboard — referral link immediately visible?
38. Marketing materials available?
39. Commission structure clear?
40. Easy to share via WhatsApp?

## Investor First Experience

### First Login
41. Dashboard — meaningful data or all zeros?
42. Opportunities — clear how to express interest?
43. Documents — relevant docs available?
44. Impact — shows real metrics?
